Local Food

Surabaya's local food scene is a gastronomic delight, a fusion of rich flavors and diverse culinary traditions. It offers a journey through the city's cultural tapestry, a feast for both the palate and the senses.

- Rujak Buah -

Spicy, sweet, and fruity Indonesian salad

- Pecel -

Surabaya's iconic spicy peanut salad, bursting with flavors.

- Rawon -

Javanese beef soup, dark from keluak nuts, intensely flavorful.

- Lontong Balap -

Surabayan dish; rice cake, tofu, bean sprouts, savory sauce.

- Tahu Campur -

A Surabayan dish with mixed tofu, bean sprouts, meat, and a savory broth, creating a harmonious blend of flavors and textures.

- Tahu Tek -

Surabayan specialty featuring fried tofu, crackers, boiled eggs, and a sweet-spicy peanut sauce, creating a delightful blend of textures and flavors.

- Rujak Cingur -

East Javanese salad with beef snout and vegetables

- Sego Sambel Mak Yeye -

Rice with spicy sambal sauce, famous for its bold and fiery flavors, often accompanied by various side dishes.

- Sate Klopo Ondomohen -

Delicacy of coconut-infused satay, rich, and flavorful, typically served with aromatic rice cakes and a tasty peanut sauce.

- Semanggi -

Surabayan dish, bean sprouts, crackers, clover leaf, and sweet-spicy peanut sauce, a flavorful and crunchy delight with a hint of heat.

- Gado Gado -

Indonesian salad, colorful mix of vegetables, tofu, tempeh, and peanut sauce, creating a delicious and satisfying medley of textures and flavors.
