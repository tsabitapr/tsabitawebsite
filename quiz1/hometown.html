Hometown

Welcome to my beloved hometown, a place where memories are woven into every street, and culture thrives around every corner. Explore its essence and discover its hidden gems with me.

Surabaya

Surabaya, East Java's capital, thrives as a metropolis with a bustling seaport, rich history, diverse economy, and vibrant culture. Iconic landmarks like Heroes Monument and Suramadu Bridge reflect its heritage and modernity, while local cuisine adds flavor to its appeal for residents and visitors.

Surabaya is my hometown, and it has been my cherished abode since the day I was born. While one might expect that a lifetime spent in the same place could breed familiarity and perhaps even boredom, I find that my affection for this city only deepens with time. Its bustling streets, vibrant markets, and the enduring warmth of its people have etched an indelible mark in my heart. Despite the allure of distant horizons, I remain steadfast in my commitment to Surabaya, for it is here that my roots are firmly planted, and here I intend to stay until the wisdom of old age graces my years.
